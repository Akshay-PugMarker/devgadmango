About Devgad Taluka and Our Organization

Devgad, the heartland of Alphonso mango, is now home to one of the most advanced and largest mango processing factory in the Konkan region of Maharashtra. Devgad Taluka Amba Utpadak Sahakari Sanstha Ltd has started one of the biggest fruit/food processing plant in the Konkan region. Located at village Dabhole in Devgad Taluka, this plant of 5 ton capacity has capabilities to process any fruits giving any output as desired.
